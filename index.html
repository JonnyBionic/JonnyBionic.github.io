My road to being a developer

Learning to code has been something that I have been interested in for a long time, but never had the time to really dive into.

I had been working as a kitchen manager for the last fifteen years and, after the coronavirus hit the United States, I lost my job. I took the tragedy and turned it into something positive by spending my time learning a new skill.

So here I am!!
